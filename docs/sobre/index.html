About

We are a group of professors, colleagues, researchers, and students with common interests in Logic, Metaphysics, and Formal Philosophy. The group is hosted at the Federal University of Ouro Preto (UFOP), but it also counts with researchers from serveral different universities. We have a weekly based schedule, and our activities include both reading sessions and invited speakers. Some of our meetings have been recorded and are available on Youtube.
